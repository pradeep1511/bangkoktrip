Places Visited

Bangkok's multi-faceted sights, attractions and city life appeal to diverse groups of tourists.

Alcazar Cabaret Show

One shall not miss a chance to see the world-famous Alcazar or Tiffany cabaret show. We will be entertained with music, lip-sings, dances and various shows played by many perfectly beautiful female actresses.

Gems Gallery

The ultimate shopping experience for jewellry products in Bangkok, Chiangmai, Phuket & Pattaya with price and quality lifetime guaranteed.Good To Visit once, and watch the collection and designs of Jewellry.

Coral Island-Koh Lann

Koh Lann offers a change of pace from the busy beaches of Pattaya - an island well known to Thai and every visitor for its tranquil surroundings and also a change of pace from the upbeat dynamism of Pattaya beaches.

Walking Street

The street is a tourist attraction that draws foreigners and Thai nationals, primarily for its night life. It also includes seafood restaurants, live music venues, beer bars, discothèques, sports bars, go-go bars, nightclubs and hotels.

Safari World and Marine Park

Thailand's greatest open zoo and leisure park that offers a great variety of entertainment for everyone to view the wild animals, feed the giraffes, enjoy the shows of Dolphins, Sealions, elephants, Orangutans, Elephant and Birds.

Goddess Buddha Temples

Buddha is considered the palladium of the Kingdom of Thailand.It is a figurine of the meditating Buddha seated in yogic posture, made of a semi-precious stone clothed in gold and housed on the grounds of the Grand Palace in Bangkok.

Special Features

Bangkok is an extreme primate city, significantly dwarfing Thailand's other urban centres in terms of importance.

One of the world's top tourist destination cities and culture of Bangkok reflects its position as Thailand's centre of wealth and modernisation.Like most of Thailand, Bangkok has a tropical savanna climate under the Köppen climate classification and is under the influence of the South Asian monsoon system.It is the economic centre of Thailand, and the heart of the country's investment and development.It is the centre of Thailand's media industry,All national newspapers, broadcast media and major publishers are based in the capital. Road-based transport is the primary mode of travel in Bangkok.

Offered Services

SpiceJet - Kolkata to Bangkok (RoundTrip)

One of the leading global carriers, Spicejet is known for offering top-notch services at economical prices.Travelled from Kolkata to Bangkok(Round Trip) and landed on Suvarnabhumi Intenational Airport(Biggest Airport saw in my life).

Hotel IBIS Pattaya

The beach beckons from one direction, the shops from the other. Streetside barbeques, bargain boutiques, famous nightlife, it is all action. And the ibis Pattaya is right in the heart of it. Enjoyed a lot with Thai,Sandwich and Oil massage and all.

Season Siam Hotel-Bangkok

Seasons Siam Hotel offers friendly and affordable comfort in the city center. The hotel has an easy commute to downtown areas of Bangkok. Breakfast provided was awesome with Chicken Sausages and Sea Bass Fish with Juices.
